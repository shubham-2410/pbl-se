About

It’s not about Who we are ? it’s about Why we are ?

Welcome to Mark Me,
the smart way to take attendance! Our website is designed to streamline the process of attendance-taking, making it quick and efficient for both students and teachers.

With our innovative system, students simply need to be in the designated location to be marked present. The website utilizes location tracking to ensure that students are physically present in the classroom, eliminating the possibility of fraudulent attendance.

To further ensure accuracy, Mark Me also uses OTP verification. When students arrive at their designated location, they will receive a unique OTP code that they must input into the website to confirm their attendance. This two-factor authentication process ensures that only the intended student is marked present.

For teachers, Mark Me is a time-saving solution that eliminates the need for manual attendance-taking. Our website automatically generates attendance reports, reducing administrative tasks and allowing teachers to focus on what really matters: teaching.

At Mark Me, we understand that every second counts in the classroom. That's why we've created a system that is reliable, efficient, and user-friendly. Join us in revolutionizing the way attendance is taken - sign up for Mark Me today!
